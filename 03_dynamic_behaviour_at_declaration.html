03 - Dynamic Behaviour is Specified at Time of Declaration

RsJS allows you to specify the "dynamic behaviour" of a value completely at the time of declaration,
meaning when a changes, b will change as well:

var streamA = Rx.Observable.of(3, 4); //future change of a is described at declaration
var streamB = streamA.map(a => 10 * a);
streamB.subscribe(b => console.log(b));
Output again to be seen in console: F12
